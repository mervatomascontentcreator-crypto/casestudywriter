How it works

The process

  1. Intake call

    We talk through the story, the outcome, and what you want to achieve with it.

  2. Client interview

    I interview your client directly via video call (30-45 min, recorded with permission). This same recording becomes the source for both the written case study and, if you've chosen Full Campaign, the video clip.

  3. First draft

    Delivered within your package's turnaround window.

  4. Revision round

    You review and request changes.

  5. Final delivery

    Polished, formatted, and ready to publish.
